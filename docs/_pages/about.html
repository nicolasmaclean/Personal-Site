I am a Computer Science and Arts, Technology, and Emerging Communications (ATEC) student at The University of Texas at Dallas.

I have always had a fascination with the ability to create. It is truly amazing to imagine something and bring it to life. This passion has led me deeper into game development and web development through programming and spread into 3D Art.

I am proficent in HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Jekyll (static website generator), and the Unity Game Engine with C#. I am also reasonably capable with Python, C++, Java, and MIPS.

I also have strong skills in 3D modeling with Maya/Blender, texturing in Substance Painter, and some experience with PhotoShop and Clip Studio Paint.
